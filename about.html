Overview

UpSports Football Agency is a newly established football agency in Ghana owned by Frank K. Asante (Licensed FIFA agent) and managed by a group of brilliant minded personnel who work round the clock for the agency. The agency's main purpose is to advice, represent players and managers in contract negotiations, transfers, Financial and brand management. The agency drives for innovation in contract negotiations with it's unique style for instance, instead of waiting for a club to approach our player, we decide which club is right for the player and then try to engineer the transfer.

Mission Statement

We strive to be Africa's most respected Sports agency brand and first choice agency for both World class athletes and promising talented athletes.

Vision

Our vision is realizing the full potential of athletes and the go-to agency for world class athlete and managers.

Guiding Principles

Integrity, Discipline, Equality and Commitment to Excellence
